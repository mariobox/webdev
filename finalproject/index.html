Common Pressure Washer Applications

Most people use a pressure washer to wash their cars. When washing your car, keep the pressure at a low setting, and take advantage of the accessories that come with the washer to give your car a great finish.

Another common application is cleaning wooden decks. For best results set your pressure washer at a moderate pressure setting, and always clean after the rainy season is over.

Cleaning driveways and concrete surfaces where mold has set in requires a strong pressure washer at a high pressure setting. With the right tool for the job this task will be completed quickly and effectively

Why do you need a pressure washer?

  • A pressure washer is eco-friendly. You will save up to 80% of the water you would use with a regular hose
  • A pressure washer can give you up to 180 bars of pressure, letting you clean surfaces that would be virtually impossible to clean with a regular garden hose and a brush.
  • Accessories like the rotating nozzle and the high performance adjusting lance make any cleaning job a breeze.
  • With the modern induction motors and Aluminum pumps found in today's pressure washers there is no need for maintenance, giving you years of trouble-free service.
